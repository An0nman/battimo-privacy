Battimo — Privacy Policy

Cute emoji battery icons for your status bar

Effective date: July 4, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 4, 2026

The short version: Battimo is built to work without collecting your personal data. Your settings and saved designs stay on your device. There is no account or login. The only data leaving your phone is what's needed to load design artwork and to show ads through Google AdMob, as explained below.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Battimo mobile application (the "App", package com.cute.emoji.battery.battery_icon) handles information. Battimo personalizes your Android status bar with cute, custom battery designs. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Information we collect

We do not collect personal information that identifies you. Battimo has no account system and does not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or location.

  • On-device settings & saved designs — your chosen design, colors, sizes and other preferences are stored locally on your phone (using standard app storage). They are not transmitted to us. Uninstalling the App removes them.
  • No personal analytics of our own — we do not run our own tracking or analytics SDK to profile you.

3. Designs & the internet

Design artwork is loaded from our image host (Cloudinary) and our catalog is served from Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore), so you always see the latest designs. Loading an image or the catalog is an ordinary web request: your device's IP address is visible to the host (as with loading any image or page online), and no personal information is attached. A small, anonymous counter may be incremented in Firestore to show which designs are popular — this is not tied to you or your device identity. Design images are cached temporarily on your device and are not kept permanently.

4. Permissions we use

  • Accessibility Service — used only to draw your chosen design over the system status bar and to detect the status-bar gestures you set up. It reads only the status bar's size and position to place the overlay. It does not read, store, or share the text or content of your screen, and it does not log your activity.
  • Battery, Wi-Fi & signal status — read to display them inside your custom status bar. They are shown on your device only — never stored or sent.
  • Display over other apps / foreground service & notification — used to keep your custom status bar visible and running reliably. A small ongoing notification may be shown so Android keeps the overlay active.
  • Ignore battery optimizations — requested so the overlay is not killed by the system and your design keeps showing.
  • Network & Wi-Fi state / Internet — used to load designs and to know whether you're online (so offline you still see your saved and applied designs).
  • Vibrate — used for light haptic feedback when you tap controls.

5. Advertising

Battimo shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain device information — such as advertising identifiers, general device data, and app-interaction data — under Google's own policies. Please see Google's Advertising policy and how Google uses information from apps that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

You can limit ad personalization at any time in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads), and you can remove ads entirely by subscribing to Battimo Premium (see below).

6. Purchases (Battimo Premium)

Battimo offers an optional Premium subscription that removes ads and unlocks all premium designs. Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing — we never see or store your payment card details. Your purchase status is stored on your device and validated through Google Play so it can be restored. Subscriptions are managed and can be cancelled anytime from the Google Play Store (Play Store → Subscriptions). Google's handling of payments is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your information. We do not share personal data with third parties for their own marketing. The only third-party services involved are those described above — Google AdMob (ads), Google Firebase and Google Play Services (catalog, purchases, and core app functionality), and Cloudinary (image hosting) — each acting under its own privacy policy.

8. Data security & retention

Because Battimo keeps your preferences on your own device and doesn't maintain user accounts, there is no personal profile of you on our servers to breach. On-device data remains until you clear the app's data or uninstall it. Network requests use standard secure (HTTPS) connections.

9. Children's privacy

Battimo is a general-audience personalization app and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will address it.

10. Feedback & contact

If you contact us through the in-app Feedback option or by email, we receive your message and your email address so we can reply, and we use them only for that purpose. For any question about this policy or your data, email us at projectsbyfarhan1107@gmail.com.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. When we do, we'll change the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be reflected here at this same URL, so please check back from time to time.
